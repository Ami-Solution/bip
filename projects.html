Projects are how people make it happen

Section detailing the initiatives, programs and projects undertook (or currently undertaken) to tackle energy efficiency and carbon emission reduction. A project is a set of activities, within 1 or more buildings, with one or more people, supported by 0 or more data streams.

Wiki-style library of energy efficiency inititatives, tagged, substantiated by data, tied to buildings (optional?) and involving people (implementers, advisers)

Ranking by price / payback / complexity / type of grant / subsidy / funding, user ratings a la TripAdvisor
